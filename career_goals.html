Career Goals


When it comes to what I particularly want to do with my degree I was always left at a blank, likely because of my lack of experience. However recently I have been super motivated to work on projects that involve a visual side to them. In that way, I believe front-end work is more my style, being able to actually see and interact with what I am making rather than the "image" of what I'm making be theoretical.

This sort of work can come in many different forms, however web-design (like making this website), has been really fun and I'm having to stop myself from adding a bunch of extra designs and features for the sake of time. I also am hoping to get into game-making and possibly make a clone of a simple game in order to get a peak into that kind of world, as that interests me greatly as well. This can mean just trying to code a simple 2D platformer, or it can mean trying to get into software like Unity and Unreal Engine.

Regardless, This sort of career path really peaks my interest and I'd love to delve deeper into it and gain as much experience as I can to hopefully secure a job down this path one day, as it seems very fulfilling to work on something that people actually see and interact with.
